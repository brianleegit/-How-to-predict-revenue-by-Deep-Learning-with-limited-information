Introduction

In recent years, there has been an increasingly rapid advance in the field of machine learning which led to a large influence on business planning and business strategy for almost all industries.  Most companies and organization used machine learning to help them make better decision igain insight from their data.
The aggressive and uncertainty of market behavior in the business environment bring great challenges in company's business planning process, enterprise logistics, and supply chain management. For example,  the company may need to comprehend the accurate future demand for the production orders before it is available. Sales forecasting is one of the technologies that developed to solve these challenges. A Sales forecast predicts the value of sales over a period of time. A key aspect of sales forecast is the forecast accuracy. Inaccurate forecasts may lead to stock-outs or overstock inventories which affects the efficiency of the business decision and even result in losses for the companies  \cite{Beheshti_Kashi_2014}.  Therefore,  sales forecasting is an increasingly important part of the success and performance of companies.
