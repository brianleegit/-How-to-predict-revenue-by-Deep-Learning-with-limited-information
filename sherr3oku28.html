Introduction

In recent years, there has been an increasingly rapid advance in the field of machine-learning which led to a large influence on business planning and business strategy for almost all industries.  Machine-learning has been used to identify objects in images, translate one language to another and to help companies and organization conduct better decision makings in a data-scientific manner. There are numerous challenges to decision makings due to an aggressive and uncertainty of market behavior in the business environment.  For example,  a fashion industry that has far away production plants may need to decide the production plans before the accurate future demand it is available. Sales forecasting is one of the technologies that can utilize machine-learning to solve these challenges. A Sales forecast predicts the value of sales over a period of time. A key aspect of sales forecast is the forecast accuracy. Inaccurate forecasts may lead to stock-outs or overstock inventories which affects the efficiency of the business decision and even result in losses for the companies  \cite{Beheshti_Kashi_2014}.  Therefore,  sales forecasting is an increasingly important part of the success and performance of companies.
