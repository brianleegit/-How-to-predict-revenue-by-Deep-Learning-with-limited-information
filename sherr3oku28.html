Introduction

The aggressive and uncertainty of market behavior in the business environment bring great challenges in company's business planning process, enterprise logistics, and supply chain management. For ex,  the company may need to determine production plans before the knowledge of future demands is available. Sales forecasting is one of the technologies that developed to solve these challenges. A Sales forecast predicts the value of sales over a period of time. A key aspect of sales forecast is the forecast accuracy. Inaccurate forecasts may lead to stock-outs or overstock inventories which affects the efficiency of the business decision and even result in losses for the companies  \cite{Beheshti_Kashi_2014}.  Therefore,  sales forecasting is an increasingly important part of the success and performance of companies.
