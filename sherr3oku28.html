Introduction

The aggressive and uncertainty of market behavior in the business environment bring great challenges in company's planning process, enterprise logistics, and supply chain management. Sales forecasting is one of the technologies that developed to solve these challenges. A Sales forecast predicts the value of sales over a period of time. A f sales forecast is the forecast accuracy. Inaccurate forecasts may lead to stock-outs or overstock inventories which affects the efficiency of the company's business decision and even result in losses for the business \cite{Beheshti_Kashi_2014}.
