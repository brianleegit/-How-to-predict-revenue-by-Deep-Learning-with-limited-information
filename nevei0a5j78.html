Traditional sales forecast performance
Sales forecasting mainly depends on historical sales data. Historical sales data are classified as a time series data. Tak-chung Fu (2010) defined time series as sets of data points which takes into account of temporal ordering \cite{Fu_2011}. Traditional sales forecasting techniques are often applied using time series analysis such as Autoregressive integrated moving average (ARIMA) and regression models. More recent approaches are based on artificial neural network (ANN) models and the newest is hybrid models which combine both approaches \cite{Fabianov__2016}.
Many important human activities and natural processes can be analyzed with time series analysis especially sales.
